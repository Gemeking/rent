Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my property on Shegabet?

Property owners can create an account, verify their identity, and use our simple listing form to add properties with photos, descriptions, and pricing.

Are all properties on Shegabet verified?

Yes, we verify all property listings to ensure accuracy and authenticity. Our team reviews each submission before it goes live on our platform.

What cities in Ethiopia does Shegabet cover?

We currently cover major cities including Addis Ababa, Adama, Bahir Dar, Hawassa, Mekelle, and several others across different regions.

How can I schedule a property viewing?

After finding a property you're interested in, you can contact the owner directly through our messaging system to arrange a viewing appointment.
