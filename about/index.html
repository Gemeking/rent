About Shegabet

Your trusted platform to find and rent homes across Ethiopia

Who We Are

Shegabet is Ethiopia's premier online home rental platform, designed to make finding your perfect home simple and stress-free. Founded in 2023, we connect thousands of Ethiopians with quality rental properties across major cities including Addis Ababa, Adama, Bahir Dar, and more.

Our platform brings together property owners and tenants in a transparent, efficient marketplace. We verify all listings to ensure accuracy and provide tools that make the rental process smooth for everyone involved.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize home renting in Ethiopia by providing a platform that is accessible, reliable, and easy to use. We believe everyone deserves to find a home they love without the traditional hassles of property searching.

Whether you're looking for a single room, a family apartment, or a luxury villa, Shegabet provides powerful search tools, transparent pricing in Ethiopian Birr, and detailed property information to help you make the right decision.

Why Choose Us?

  • Wide variety of verified rental listings across Ethiopia
  • Advanced search filters by location, price, and property type
  • Secure platform with verified property owners
  • Transparent pricing in Ethiopian Birr
  • Dedicated customer support team
  • Mobile-friendly design for on-the-go searching
